  • Sources: iOS App Store (46 reviews), Google Play Store (288 reviews)
  • Coverage: Global reviews in 15+ languages

Analysis Period

  • Start Date: June 19, 2025
  • End Date: June 26, 2025
  • Total Duration: 7 days

Categorization Method

  • Categories: Positive feedback, Feature requests, Issues/complaints, Competitive mentions, General feedback
  • Language Processing: Manual analysis with translation for non-English reviews
  • Sentiment Scoring: Based on star ratings and content analysis

Limitations

  • Sample Size: 334 reviews over 7 days (limited time window)
  • Platform Coverage: App stores only (no Reddit, Twitter, or other social media)
  • Regional Bias: Higher representation from certain geographic regions

Confidence Level

  • High Confidence: Major themes (uBlock Origin success, Collections issues)
  • Medium Confidence: Feature request priorities and regional preferences
  • Sample Diversity: Good coverage across platforms, languages, and rating ranges
